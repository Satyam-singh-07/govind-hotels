About Govind Residency

A premium hotel offering comfortable accommodations with modern amenities in the heart of Greater Noida.

Your Comfort, Our Priority

Located in the prime area of Greater Noida, Govind Residency offers well-appointed rooms with modern amenities for both business and leisure travelers.

Our hotel features clean, comfortable rooms with attached bathrooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, 24/7 security, and excellent service to ensure a memorable stay.

Whether you're visiting for business, tourism, or a short stay, we provide the perfect accommodation with convenient access to local attractions and business centers.
